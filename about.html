About Us

Domfete Society — Methodist Church Ghana

Our Story:

The Domfete Society of the Methodist Church Ghana is a vibrant and growing Christian community within the Jinijini Circuit, dedicated to spreading the Gospel of Jesus Christ and transforming lives through worship, service, and fellowship.Over the years, the Society has become a strong spiritual home for believers in the Domfete community and its surrounding areas.

Mission:

To bring people to the saving knowledge of Christ, nurture believers, and impact the community through evangelism and service.

Vision:

To be a spiritually mature, united and mission-focused Society that transforms lives.

Spiritual life includes Sunday divine services, Bible studies, youth meetings, and outreach programs. The Church is committed to supporting education, health awareness, and community outreach.

Core Values:

.Faithfulness - standing the in word of God
.Love- Demonstrating Chrislike compassion to all
. Unity-Working together as one Body in Christ
.Serving God and humanity with humility
.Discipline-Upholding Methodist doctrines and order
